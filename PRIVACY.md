# Privacy Policy — Cofr

**Effective date:** April 6, 2026

Cofr is a personal finance app developed for personal use only.

## Data Collection

Cofr does not collect, share, or sell any personal data to third parties. All financial data accessed through Apple FinanceKit remains on your device or is stored in your personal backend infrastructure, which you control entirely.

## Apple FinanceKit

Cofr uses Apple FinanceKit solely to read Apple Card transaction data for personal budgeting and spending analysis. This data is not transmitted to any third party.

## Contact

This app is not distributed publicly. For questions, contact the developer directly.
